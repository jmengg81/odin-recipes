Saba Teshoku Recipe

This recipe is a healthy and easy addition to your cooking arsenal. Preparation is simple and the rewards ample. This is a popular dish in Japan for any meal, breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Rice
  • Miso paste
  • Oil
  • Water

Cooking Steps

  1. Wash the rice twice in water, then place in rice cooker with equal parts water (1 cup of water per cup of rice), and start the cooker
  2. Heat up oil in a pan and fry the fish until crispy and brown on both sides and cooked in the middle
  3. Boil 2/3 cups of water and add a tea sppon of miso paste to it and stir in
